# Sign Up

Visit our official site [https://api.ikuncode.cc/](https://api.ikuncode.cc/). You can register with a custom account name, or use one of the supported third-party sites for quick sign-up.

## Registration Steps

1. Open the official site
2. Click the "Sign Up" button
3. Choose a registration method:
   - Register with email
   - Quick sign-up via a third-party account

![Sign-up page](/images/tu1.png)

## Registration Methods

### Email Registration
- Enter your email address
- Set a password
- Receive a verification email
- Complete registration

### Quick Sign-up
Quick registration is supported via:
- LinuxDo

::: tip 💡 Tip
We recommend registering with email — it makes password recovery and receiving notifications much easier.
:::

## After Registration

Once registered, you can:
1. [Create your own API Key](/guide/create-key)
2. Start using AI coding tools

## FAQ

### Didn't receive the verification email?
- Check your spam folder
- Confirm the email address is entered correctly
- Wait 1–2 minutes and try again
- Contact support for help

### Forgot your password?
On the login page, click "Forgot password" and reset it via your registered email.
