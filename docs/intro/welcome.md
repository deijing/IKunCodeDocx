# Welcome to IKunCode!

IKunCode is an API relay focused on boosting developer productivity (currently primarily for Claude Code, CodeX, and similar services). We stand by our values of *sincerity and passion*, and we hope to build genuine connections with our users. If anything feels off or you run into trouble, please reach out to our support team directly (see [Pre-sales & After-sales](/support/after-sales)).

::: tip 💡 Tip
We offer an out-of-the-box service — no complex setup required to enjoy a high-quality AI coding assistant.
:::

## Why Choose IKunCode?

### 🛠️ Professionally Operated
